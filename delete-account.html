ISHARE – Account Deletion Request

Last updated: March 2026

About ISHARE

ISHARE is a ride and cost-sharing mobile application that connects people traveling in the same direction in Rwanda. This page explains how users can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your ISHARE account, please send an email to:

Email: ishareapp16@gmail.com
Subject: ISHARE Account Deletion Request

Include the following in your email:

  • Your registered phone number or email address
  • Your full name as used in the app
  • A short message stating: “I request deletion of my ISHARE account and associated data.”

We may request additional verification to confirm account ownership.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile information (name, phone number, email)
  • Trip postings and bookings
  • Ride history
  • Ratings and reviews (may be anonymized)
  • Support communications linked to your account

Data That May Be Retained

Limited information may be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, security investigations, or financial/accounting obligations. Retained data is stored securely and not used for marketing.

Deletion Timeframe

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days after verification.
